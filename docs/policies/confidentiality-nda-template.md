# Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Template

## TLDR

NDA template for all Helix Stax employees, contractors, and third parties with access to confidential information. Governed by Virginia law (VUTSA). Covers client data, technical information, and business information. Approved by CEO.

---

## Purpose

This agreement protects Helix Stax confidential information, client data, and intellectual property by establishing legally binding non-disclosure obligations for all personnel and third parties.

## Scope

- All employees, contractors, interns, and third parties with access to Helix Stax systems or data
- Jurisdiction: Commonwealth of Virginia
- Enforcement: Virginia Uniform Trade Secrets Act (VUTSA)

---

## Agreement Terms

### 1. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

- **Client Data:** Any information stored within or retrieved from client infrastructure (databases, codebases, logs, PHI/PII)
- **Technical Information:** Source code, architectural diagrams, security configurations, API keys, and internal IP
- **Business Information:** Pricing, client lists, strategic plans, and financial data
- **Exclusions:** Information that is or becomes public knowledge through no fault of the Recipient

### 2. Non-Disclosure Obligations

The Recipient agrees to:

1. Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
2. Use Confidential Information solely for the performance of professional duties for Helix Stax
3. Not copy, distribute, or reverse-engineer any Confidential Information without express written consent
4. Implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, MFA) when handling such information

### 3. Intellectual Property (IP) Ownership

- **Work for Hire:** All work product, code, documentation, and innovations created during the term of employment/contract related to Helix Stax business are "works made for hire" and sole property of Helix Stax.
- **Assignment:** To the extent any IP does not qualify as work for hire, the Recipient assigns all right, title, and interest to Helix Stax.

### 4. Permitted Disclosures

The Recipient may disclose Confidential Information if required by a valid court order or government subpoena. The Recipient must provide Helix Stax with immediate notice to allow the firm to seek a protective order, unless prohibited by law.

### 5. Return of Materials

Upon request or termination:

1. Return all physical documents and hardware containing Confidential Information
2. Permanently delete all electronic copies from personal devices or cloud storage
3. Certify in writing that all materials have been returned or destroyed

### 6. Term and Survival

- This Agreement is effective as of the date of signing and continues throughout the Recipient's engagement.
- **Trade Secret obligations survive indefinitely.** For other Confidential Information, obligations survive for **5 years** post-termination.

### 7. Remedies (Virginia Law)

The Recipient acknowledges that a breach may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages are inadequate. Helix Stax is entitled to seek injunctive relief in any court of competent jurisdiction within the Commonwealth of Virginia, in addition to any other remedies available under the Virginia Uniform Trade Secrets Act.
